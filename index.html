Project 1

I am in the university organization EWB(Engineers without borders) and was a part of the electrical team within the club. The purpose of the club during my freshman year was to build a clinic in Ulloh Ghana for the less fortunate. As a part of the electrical group, we were in charge of designing the electrical grid of the clinic. fans, outlets, lights etc. Within the electrical group, I was in charge of the lights. I did research on certain types of bulbs to use for effectiveness and costness. Then I designed where the lights will go in each room and how much for each room, to provide to the electrical grid for the clinic. This project provided an insight into a new skill because of to this point, I had done very little electrical work, and it showed me the importance of working together in a group.
